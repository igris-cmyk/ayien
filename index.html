Igris is one of the most powerful and loyal shadow soldiers in the web novel "Solo Leveling". As a former knight, he retains his chivalrous demeanor and exceptional combat skills. Igris serves under the protagonist, Sung Jin-Woo, and is often seen leading other shadows into battle. His unwavering loyalty and formidable strength make him an invaluable asset in Jin-Woo's quest to become the strongest hunter.
